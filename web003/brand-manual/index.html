Core Principles

Typography-First

The typeface (Trirong) carries the entire identity. No symbols or icons are used in the wordmark. Typography and whitespace lead the design.

Restrained Color

Color supports content but never dominates. CNX Navy is the primary brand signal. Accent colors are used sparingly (maximum 10% of visual space).

Generous Whitespace

Content breathes. Minimum margins of 25mm for print materials. Ample spacing between elements creates an institutional, professional presence.

Institutional Tone

The brand communicates like a law firm or elite advisory practice—formal, measured, confident. Every design choice should pass the "respected advisory firm" test.
