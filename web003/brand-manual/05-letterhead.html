Letterhead

Official Letterhead Template and Specifications

The CNX Concierge letterhead maintains institutional formality while allowing content to lead. The wordmark appears at the top, with contact information in the footer. Generous margins ensure the document breathes and maintains professional presence.

Specifications

Paper

Stock: Premium uncoated

Weight: 100–120gsm minimum

Color: Natural white or cream

Finish: Matte or soft-touch

Dimensions

Standard: A4 (210 × 297mm)

US Letter: 8.5 × 11 inches

Margins: 25mm minimum all sides

Header space: 40mm from top

Colors

Wordmark: CNX Navy (#0c2b5c)

Text: Ink (#1a1a1a)

Accent: Lavender (#76235f) optional

Background: White or natural paper

Usage Guidelines

Header Requirements

  • Wordmark must appear in CNX Navy (#0c2b5c)
  • Minimum 40mm from top edge
  • Separator line: 6px Lavender accent border
  • No symbols or icons in header

Footer Requirements

  • Contact information in Harbor Gray
  • Company name in CNX Navy (bold)
  • Minimum 15mm from bottom edge
  • Separator line: 6px Lavender accent border

Content Area

  • Minimum 25mm margins on all sides
  • Body text in Ink (#1a1a1a)
  • Line height: 1.7 for readability
  • Generous spacing between paragraphs

Print Specifications

  • CMYK color space for print
  • 300 DPI minimum resolution
  • Bleed: 3mm if required
  • Safe area: 5mm from edges
