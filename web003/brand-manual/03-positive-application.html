Positive Application

Brand Application on Light Backgrounds

Positive application refers to the standard brand presentation on light backgrounds. This is the primary application method used for most materials, including websites, printed documents, and standard communications.

Color Application

Wordmark

CNX CONCIERGE

Wordmark appears in CNX Navy (#0c2b5c) on light backgrounds (CNX Light Paper or White).

Headings

Primary Heading

Secondary Heading

All headings (H1, H2, H3) use CNX Navy for brand consistency and hierarchy.

Body Text

Body text uses Ink (#1a1a1a) for optimal readability on light backgrounds. This provides maximum contrast and ensures comfortable reading for long-form content.

Body text always uses Ink color, never CNX Navy, to maintain readability.

Secondary Text

Captions, metadata, and secondary information use Harbor Gray (#59647a) to establish hierarchy without competing with primary content.

Harbor Gray supports the hierarchy by providing visual distinction for less critical information.

Links and Accents

For more information, contact CNX Concierge.

Links and interactive elements use Lavender Accent (#76235f) to signal interactivity while maintaining sophistication.

Usage Guidelines

Primary Use Cases

  • Website and digital interfaces
  • Printed documents and letterhead
  • Business cards and stationery
  • Marketing materials
  • Presentations and reports

Best Practices

  • Always maintain sufficient contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for body text)
  • Use CNX Navy for wordmark and headings only
  • Reserve Ink for body text to ensure readability
  • Use Harbor Gray for secondary information
  • Apply Lavender Accent sparingly (maximum 10% of visual space)
