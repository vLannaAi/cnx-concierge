Business Card

Front and Back Designs with Specifications

The CNX Concierge business card maintains institutional elegance through restrained design. The front features the wordmark and individual information, while the back provides contact details. Two-color printing (CNX Navy + Lavender Accent) creates sophistication without excess.

Specifications

Dimensions

Standard: 85 × 55mm (ISO 7810)

US Standard: 89 × 51mm (3.5 × 2 inches)

Bleed: 3mm all sides

Safe area: 5mm from edges

Stock

Weight: 350gsm minimum

Type: Uncoated or soft-touch matte

Finish: No gloss

Optional: Letterpress or deboss for wordmark

Colors

Primary: CNX Navy (#0c2b5c)

Accent: Lavender (#76235f)

Text: Ink (#1a1a1a) or White

Background: White or CNX Navy

Content Guidelines

Front Side

  • Wordmark: CNX CONCIERGE (required)
  • Name: Full name, font-weight 600
  • Title: Professional title or role
  • Department: Optional, in Lavender accent
  • Keep information minimal and focused

Back Side

  • Wordmark: CNX CONCIERGE (optional, smaller)
  • Contact: Email and phone number
  • Website: cnxconcierge.com
  • Organize information in clear sections

Typography

  • Font: Trirong (all text)
  • Wordmark: 0.9rem, bold, 0.1em letter-spacing
  • Name: 0.75rem, semi-bold
  • Title: 0.65rem, regular
  • Contact info: 0.55rem, regular

Print Requirements

  • CMYK color space
  • 300 DPI minimum resolution
  • 3mm bleed on all sides
  • 5mm safe area from edges
  • Consider letterpress or deboss for wordmark
