Approach

Voyagers

Who we serve

We call our clients Voyagers. They are defined by intention, not just movement. They cross borders to participate, not consume. Like Marco Polo, whose journeys created pathways for trade and connection, today's Voyagers build bridges that algorithms cannot replicate. They choose presence over abstraction. CNX Concierge exists to serve this purpose.

The Art of Anticipation

How we work

The finest concierge never waits to be asked. We anticipate needs, prepare solutions, and execute with discretion. Whether arranging a first apartment or structuring a company, we ensure every detail is handled so the Voyager's attention remains on what matters most.

The CNX Gateway

Our territory

CNX represents the greater Chiang Mai region: Chiang Mai Province, Lamphun, and the surrounding Lanna highlands. This is our territory. We know its rhythms, networks, and opportunities. CNX Concierge serves as the trusted gateway to this region.

Services

CNX Concierge provides complete support for international professionals in Northern Thailand. Our services span the full journey: from first arrival to established operations. We begin with the practical and evolve toward the strategic as your commitment deepens.

Planning

Pre-arrival preparation

Free pre-arrival assessment on request, with advance notice. We review your requirements, match them with opportunities, and help you prepare documentation and certifications on time for flawless execution.
This is anticipation.

Landing

First steps in Thailand

Visa processing (DTV, extensions, 90-day reports), airport reception, initial orientation.

Business visa support, BOI eligibility assessment, company registration preparation.

Grounding

Establishing a base

Accommodation search, bank account setup, mobile and internet services, coworking access, healthcare navigation.

Company formation, BOI application support, startup registration, obtaining licenses to operate.

Building

Long-term operations

Company formation, legal structuring, Employer of Record (EOR), local director services, partnership introductions, business process outsourcing (BPO) support.

We do not publish fixed pricing. Every engagement is scoped to the specific situation. Initial consultations are without obligation. Contact us to begin a conversation.

Partner Network

Our service delivery is supported by a network of vetted local partners: Thai legal counsel for corporate and immigration matters, licensed accounting practices for tax compliance, and certified translation services for document processing.
