Palette 1: CNX Ink & Paper

Neutral primary system

A restrained neutral palette built around Ink and Paper. Color steps back so typography, whitespace, and content lead. This palette preserves maximum continuity with the existing CNX Concierge system while remaining suitable for both web and print.

Ink
#1a1a1a
Primary text and wordmark. Used for CNX Concierge brand, headings, and high-contrast body text.
Paper
#faf9f7
Background and negative space. Provides a soft, print-like canvas for long-form reading.
Warm Gray
#6b7c8a
Secondary text, captions, labels, and rules. Supports hierarchy without competing with primary text.
Deep Gold
#6b5a3f
Functional accents only: links, subtle dividers, and rare emphasis. Never used as a background flood.

Palette 2: CNX Deep Navy

Navy-led primary system

A navy-led palette where CNX Navy carries the wordmark and primary headings. This option shifts the brand toward a more institutional, banking-style presence while maintaining neutrality and restraint.

CNX Navy
#0c2b5c
Primary brand color. Used for the CNX Concierge wordmark, primary navigation, and key headings.
CNX Light Paper
#f7f8fb
Soft, cool background for sections and panels. Keeps navy feeling grounded, not heavy.
Harbor Gray
#59647a
Secondary text and dividers. Provides depth without introducing a new hue family.
Lavender Accent
#76235f
Reserved for links, small callouts, and subtle UI elements. Adds subtle sophistication without competing with navy.

Palette 3: CNX Lanna Dusk

Cool neutrals with warm accent

A dusk-inspired palette that pairs deep blue-gray ink with soft, stone backgrounds and a restrained terracotta accent. This option introduces more atmosphere while keeping the brand firmly in institutional territory.

Dusk Ink
#2d4158
Primary ink for headings and selected wordmark use. Dark enough for long-form text, rich enough to feel deliberate.
Stone Paper
#f3f2ee
Section backgrounds and cards. Slight warmth keeps the palette human and print-like.
Mist Gray
#7c8896
Secondary text, captions, table rules. Used where hierarchy is needed without strong contrast.
Terracotta Accent
#8b6347
Accent for links, data points, and subtle highlights. Warm earth tone that complements the dusk palette.
