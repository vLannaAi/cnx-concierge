Proposal 1: Classical Serif Authority

Noto Serif + Noto Serif Thai

A classical, scholarly serif typeface that embodies institutional gravitas and timeless elegance. Noto Serif provides exceptional readability and a refined character that aligns with law firm and elite advisory practice aesthetics. Native Thai script support ensures seamless bilingual communication.

Font Information

Primary Font: Noto Serif

Thai Support: Noto Serif Thai (native)

Character: Classical, scholarly, timeless, institutional

Google Fonts: Noto Serif | Noto Serif Thai

Rationale: Matches law firm and institutional aesthetic. The serif formality conveys established expertise and professional discretion, while maintaining excellent legibility across all media.

Proposal 3: Editorial Serif Elegance

Cormorant Garamond + Noto Sans Thai

A high-contrast, editorial serif typeface that combines literary elegance with professional gravitas. Cormorant Garamond offers distinctive character and refined authority, perfect for brands that value both sophistication and readability. Font Information

Primary Font: Cormorant Garamond

Character: High contrast, editorial authority, refined, literary elegance

Rationale: Literary elegance with professional gravitas. The high contrast and editorial character convey sophistication while maintaining excellent readability. Ideal for brands that want to stand apart with distinctive typography while remaining professional.

Proposal 4: Traditional Thai Serif Authority

Trirong

A serif typeface with native Thai script support, featuring a narrow, tall structure reminiscent of traditional Thai typography. Trirong combines thick and thin strokes with oval-shaped looped terminals, creating a formal, institutional appearance. The typeface's space-efficient design maintains excellent readability while honoring Thai typographic heritage.

Font Information

Primary Font: Trirong

Thai Support: Native (integrated Thai and Latin scripts)

Character: Traditional Thai serif, formal, narrow structure, institutional

Google Fonts: Trirong

Rationale: Combines institutional formality with authentic Thai typographic heritage. The narrow, tall structure and traditional looped terminals create a distinctive identity that honors the Lanna region's cultural context while maintaining professional gravitas suitable for law firm and advisory practice aesthetics.

Proposal 5: Modern Thai Serif Elegance

Taviraj

A serif typeface with native Thai script support, featuring a wide structure that enhances readability and legibility. Taviraj combines thick and thin strokes with rounded, airy looped terminals, creating a modern yet formal aesthetic. The typeface's generous proportions and balanced design make it ideal for professional documents requiring both elegance and clarity.

Font Information

Primary Font: Taviraj

Thai Support: Native (integrated Thai and Latin scripts)

Character: Modern Thai serif, wide structure, readable, formal elegance

Google Fonts: Taviraj

Rationale: Offers modern elegance with exceptional readability. The wide structure and rounded terminals create an approachable yet professional character, suitable for institutional use while maintaining cultural authenticity through native Thai script integration. Ideal for brands seeking contemporary sophistication with traditional roots.
