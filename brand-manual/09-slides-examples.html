Slide Design Guidelines

Layout Principles

  • Maintain generous whitespace (minimum 20% of slide area)
  • Use consistent margins (2–4rem on all sides)
  • Limit content to 5–7 bullet points per slide
  • Keep text concise and scannable
  • Ensure readability at presentation distance

Typography

  • Title: 2–2.5rem, CNX Navy, bold
  • Headings: 1.5–2rem, CNX Navy, semi-bold
  • Body text: 1–1.2rem, Ink, regular
  • Minimum font size: 18px for readability
  • Line height: 1.6–1.8 for body text

Color Application

  • Background: White or CNX Light Paper
  • Headings: CNX Navy for brand consistency
  • Body text: Ink for readability
  • Charts: Use palette colors (Navy, Lavender, Harbor Gray)
  • Avoid color overload—restraint is key

Chart Guidelines

  • Use CNX Navy as primary chart color
  • Lavender Accent for secondary data series
  • Harbor Gray for tertiary elements
  • Maintain sufficient contrast for readability
  • Include clear labels and legends
