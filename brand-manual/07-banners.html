Banners

Vertical, Horizontal, and Square Formats

Banner designs maintain brand consistency across different formats while allowing flexibility for various applications. The wordmark is prominent, with minimal text and generous whitespace. CNX Navy is the primary color, with negative application (white on navy) used for high-impact displays.

Specifications

Vertical Banner

Dimensions: 600 × 1800px

Aspect Ratio: 1:3 (portrait)

Use: Event displays, signage, social media stories

Horizontal Banner

Dimensions: 1800 × 600px

Aspect Ratio: 3:1 (landscape)

Use: Website headers, presentations, digital displays

Square Banner

Dimensions: 1200 × 1200px

Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square)

Use: Social media posts, profile images, print materials

Usage Guidelines

Design Principles

  • Wordmark must be prominent and clearly visible
  • Maintain generous whitespace (minimum 20% of area)
  • Keep text minimal—banners are not for detailed information
  • Use negative application (white on navy) for high impact
  • Ensure readability at intended viewing distance

Color Application

  • Primary: CNX Navy background with white text
  • Alternative: Light background with CNX Navy wordmark
  • Accent: Lavender used sparingly (maximum 5% of area)
  • Never use gradients or color overlays

Typography

  • Wordmark: Trirong, bold, 0.1em letter-spacing
  • Supporting text: Trirong, regular or semi-bold
  • Minimum font size: 16px for readability
  • Line height: 1.6–1.8 for multi-line text

Technical Requirements

  • RGB color space for digital displays
  • CMYK for print applications
  • 300 DPI minimum for print
  • 72–150 DPI for digital displays
  • Consider responsive sizing for web use
